TV

1. Display Technology: LED, QLED, OLED, or Mini-LED.
2. Resolution: Full HD (1920 x 1080), 4K UHD (3840 x 2160), or 8K (7680 x 4320).
3. Screen Size: Ranges from 32 inches to over 85 inches.
4. HDR Support: HDR10, Dolby Vision, HLG (Hybrid Log-Gamma).
5. Smart Features: Built-in operating systems like Android TV, webOS, Tizen OS, or proprietary systems.
7. Processor: Varies based on manufacturer, usually for handling smart features.
8. RAM/Storage: Limited RAM for multitasking, minimal internal storage for apps.
9. Connectivity: HDMI ports (multiple), USB ports, Ethernet port, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth.
10. Sound: Built-in speakers, often with support for Dolby Atmos or DTS
11. Remote Control: Standard remote with voice control capabilities in some models.
12. Design: Slim bezels, sleek design, wall-mountable.
13. Additional Features: Voice assistants (Google Assistant, Alexa), gaming modes, screen mirroring, ambient mode, and parental controls.
