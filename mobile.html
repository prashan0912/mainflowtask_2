Mobiles

1. Operating System: Commonly Android (Google) or iOS (Apple).
2. Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon, MediaTek, Apple A-series chip.
3. Memory (RAM): Typically 4 GB to 8 GB.
4. Storage: Usually starting from 64 GB, with options up to 512 GB or higher.
5. Display: Varied sizes, typically 5 to 6.5 inches, with AMOLED or LCD panels.
6. Camera: Dual or triple rear cameras, with resolutions ranging from 12 MP to 108 MP.
7. Battery: Capacity typically between 3000 mAh to 5000 mAh.
8. Connectivity: 4G/5G, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, NFC.
9. Ports: USB-C for charging and data transfer, some may retain a headphone jack.
10. Biometrics: Fingerprint scanner, Face ID, or both.
11. Build: Glass or metal body, often with water and dust resistance.
12. Operating System Updates: Regular updates provided by the manufacturer.
13. Additional Features: Wireless charging, fast charging, reverse wireless charging, stereo speakers, and IP rating for water and dust resistance.
