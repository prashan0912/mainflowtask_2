Laptop

1. Processor: Commonly Intel Core i3/i5/i7 or AMD Ryzen 3/5/7.
2. Memory: Standard 8 GB DDR4 RAM, often upgradeable.
3. Storage: Usually 256 GB SSD, upgradeable to 512 GB.
4. Graphics: Integrated Intel UHD/Iris Xe or AMD Vega graphics.
5. Display: 13-15.6 inches, Full HD resolution (1920 x 1080), IPS panel.
6. Battery Life: Typically 6-8 hours on average.
7. Ports: USB (Type-A and Type-C), HDMI, audio jack.
8 .Operating System: Often Windows 10/11, may offer Linux or Chrome OS.
9. Build: Plastic or aluminum construction, weight around 1.5-2.5 kg.
10. Keyboard: Full-sized chiclet keyboard.
11. Touchpad: Multi-touch for navigation.
12. Webcam/Audio: Built-in webcam, integrated stereo speakers.
13. Cooling: Standard fan-based cooling system.
14. Connectivity: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth.
15. Optional: Dedicated GPU, larger RAM/storage configurations.
